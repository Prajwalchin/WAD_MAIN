Welcome to Pune Institute of Computer Technology

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (abbreviated as PICT), is a private engineering college located in Dhankawadi, Pune, India. Established by the Society for Computer Technology and Research (SCTR) in 1983, it offers courses in Computer Engineering, Information Technology, and Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.
